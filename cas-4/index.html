About

Today's restaurant is theater on a grand scale.

Set in a hundred year-old townhouse in the heart of the West Village, Papa’s opened in 2007 and is one of Carlos Suarez's Casa NelaTM restaurants Stars1, that include the Village hits Rosemary's, Claudette, and Rosemary's Pizza. Like its sister restaurants, Papa’s embraces its residential setting, paying homage to La Maison de Campagne - a neighborhood restaurant inspired by nature. The restaurant's cellar offers French wines from small and interesting producers alongside more well known houses, with a selection of excellent wines for everyday drinking. The bar's seasonal cocktail lists have been celebrated since the opening in 2007, and today focus on using French products in novel ways to create modern classics like the Papa’s Piscine.

Papa’s offering includes:
  • Food and drinks
  • Wedding Catering - decorations, dinner, dessert
  • Social event cateing - from birthday to retirement parties, offering include appetizers, bartenders and balloons
